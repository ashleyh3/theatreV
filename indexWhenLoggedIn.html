Features

TheatreV is an shareable VR experience that creates a virtual private space within public events. Upon opening TheatreVR, a virtual house is created with a customizable number of rooms, each hosting unique events for yourself and anyone invited. Each room immerses the user in a unique event, allowing users to:

  • Enjoy concerts, political rallies, music festivals, and other events
  • Watch sports games from a front row perspective or a player's POV
  • Experience what you missed or relive it (Gatsby was ahead of his time -- now you can repeat the past)
  • Upload your own home movies to share moments with friends more vividly
  • Visit famous places without leaving your home through live streams taken by 3D cameras
  • Avoid crowds if the user has mental or physical health reasons, including social anxiety
  • Eliminate travel costs, making events more accessible to all interested people
  • Virtually be in two places at the same time

Better yet, you can SHARE any event with your friends! Through our multi-user mode, you can invite your friends to your house to synchronously experience the same content. With the help of motion sensors, you can watch their dance moves next to you, or watch them jump up with joy as your team wins.

Techonologies:

    Built in Unity 5.3.3
    Tested with Oculus SDK2
    Supported by Microsoft Kinect
    Also integrates LEAP Motion sensors Node.js server deployed on Azure

Future Plans

We plan to spread access for nearly every form of entertainment, with ventures into business and education. The next step as a company is enhancing our video library by collecting existing 3D videos and potentially creating a network of 3D cameras for current streams. We hope that removing physical limitations will free our users from the stresses of transportation. In many ways, our virtual private event space is allowing anyone to be an invisible teleporter, use it as you wish.
